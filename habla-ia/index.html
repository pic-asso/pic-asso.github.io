Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026.

01 What we collect

When you use HablaIA, we store your Telegram user ID, username, the language(s) you are learning, your CEFR level, vocabulary progress, grammar history, lesson count, and subscription status.

02 What we do not collect

We do not store your phone number, email address, real name, or any payment details. Payment is processed entirely by Stripe — we only receive a confirmation that a subscription was activated.

03 AI processing

Text you send during lessons is processed by Anthropic's Claude API to generate lesson content. Anthropic's own privacy policy applies to that processing. We do not store the full content of your lesson conversations — only the vocabulary and progress data extracted from them.

04 Data storage

Data is stored in a SQLite database on a Hetzner VPS located in Germany (EU). It is retained for as long as your account is active.

05 Your rights

Under GDPR you have the right to access, rectify, or delete your data at any time. To exercise any of these rights, message @pic_asso on Telegram with your Telegram user ID. Requests are handled within 30 days.

06 Contact

Data controller: Aitor López Hernández. Contact: @pic_asso on Telegram.
